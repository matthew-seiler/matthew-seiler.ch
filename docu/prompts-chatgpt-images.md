# Bild-Prompts für ChatGPT Images 2.0

Diese Prompts in ChatGPT einfügen (Thinking Mode für beste Qualität).
Bilder als JPG in höchster Auflösung herunterladen.
Images 2.0 unterstützt bis 2K/4K, verschiedene Seitenverhältnisse,
bis zu 8 Bilder pro Prompt, und nahezu perfektes Text-Rendering.


## BILD 1: Landingpage-Hintergrund (bg-landing.jpg)

```
Create a breathtaking deep space panorama as a website background image.
The scene shows a vast cosmic landscape viewed from the edge of a nebula.

Key elements:
- Deep dark background (near black, hex #0a0e1a) so white text is
  clearly readable on top
- A stunning nebula in cool blue and teal tones (hex #7eb8da as accent
  color) occupying the lower-right area, with wispy gas clouds fading
  into the darkness
- Scattered stars of varying brightness and size across the entire image,
  some with subtle glow halos
- One distant spiral galaxy visible as a small but detailed feature
  in the upper portion
- A few faint shooting stars or meteor trails adding dynamic movement
- The overall mood is vast, mysterious, and awe-inspiring
- The image must be relatively dark overall (especially the center and
  left side) because text and UI elements will be overlaid on top

Style: photorealistic astrophotography, similar to Hubble or James Webb
Space Telescope imagery. No cartoon elements, no illustrations.

Aspect ratio: 16:9 landscape
Resolution: highest available (2K or 4K)

Important: Do NOT include any text, watermarks, logos, or UI elements.
This is purely a background image.
```


## BILD 2: Blog-Detailseiten-Hintergrund (bg-blog.jpg)

```
Create a dramatic deep space scene as a website background image,
different from but complementary to a nebula panorama.

Key elements:
- Very dark base (near black, hex #0a0e1a) for contrast with a white
  paper overlay that will float on top
- A mesmerizing view looking down into the accretion disk of a distant
  black hole, with matter spiraling inward in glowing blue and
  orange-gold streams
- The black hole is positioned in the lower portion of the image, with
  gravitational lensing effect bending starlight around it
- Above the black hole, deep space continues with scattered stars
- Subtle cosmic dust particles floating in the foreground, slightly
  out of focus, creating depth
- The center of the image should be especially dark (this is where a
  white "paper" element will float on the website)
- Edges can be more dramatic with more light effects
- Faint wormhole-like distortion visible in one area, as if space
  itself is bending

Style: cinematic sci-fi realism, inspired by Interstellar and James Webb
imagery combined. Photorealistic, not illustrated.

Aspect ratio: 9:16 portrait (vertical – this page has a tall scrolling
paper element on it)
Resolution: highest available (2K or 4K)

Important: Do NOT include any text, watermarks, logos, or UI elements.
```


## BILD 3 (Optional): Tile-Vorschaubild (tile-universum.jpg)

```
Create a cinematic thumbnail image for a blog post about the most
incredible objects in the universe.

Key elements:
- A composite cosmic scene showing multiple extraordinary space objects:
  - A neutron star (pulsar) emitting twin beams of blue light
  - A small but visible black hole with accretion disk in the background
  - A wormhole-like space distortion, showing a tunnel through spacetime
    with light bending around its edges
  - A cluster of stars partially enclosed by geometric alien
    megastructures (Dyson sphere concept)
- The composition should feel like a movie poster for a space documentary
- Dark background with vibrant accent lighting in blue (#7eb8da),
  teal, and warm gold
- Dramatic lighting with rim-light effects on the objects
- Sense of enormous scale and wonder

Style: cinematic concept art meets astrophotography. Detailed, dramatic.

Aspect ratio: 16:10 landscape (used as a card/tile preview image)
Resolution: highest available

Important: Do NOT include any text, titles, or watermarks.
```


## Tipps für ChatGPT Images 2.0

1. Nutze den Thinking Mode (braucht Plus/Pro) für die beste Qualität.
2. Wenn ein Bild nicht ganz passt: beschreibe was du ändern willst.
   Images 2.0 kann Bilder iterativ verfeinern ohne den Kontext zu verlieren.
3. Lade die Bilder in höchster verfügbarer Auflösung herunter.
4. Benenne sie exakt: bg-landing.jpg, bg-blog.jpg, tile-universum.jpg.
5. Lege sie in den images/ Ordner des Projekts.
6. Falls die Dateien sehr gross sind (>2MB): in einem Bildeditor auf
   ca. 1920px Breite (Landscape) bzw. 1080px Breite (Portrait) verkleinern
   und als JPG mit 85% Qualität speichern.
